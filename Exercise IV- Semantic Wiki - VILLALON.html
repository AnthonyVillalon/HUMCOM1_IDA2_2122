Gameplay:

  • Dive into Wild Rift: the skills-and-strats 5v5 MOBA experience of League of Legends, now built from the ground up for mobile and console. With fresh controls and fast-paced games, players of every level can team up with friends, lock in their champion, and go for the big plays.

Objectives:

  1. PICK YOUR CHAMPION.
  2. CHOOSE YOUR LANE.
  3. TAKE ON THE JUNGLE.
  4. DESTROY TURRETS TO REACH NEXUS.
  5. DESTROY THE ENEMY BASE.

Release:

  • Riot and League of Legends finally released Wild Rift for gamers, a long-awaited time since the game is massively anticipated. Wild Rift is an online arena game designed for Android and iOS users so they can live the League of Legends experience on the go.
