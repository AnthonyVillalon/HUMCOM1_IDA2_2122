GUIDE ON HOW TO PROPERLY TURN OFF COMPUTER

PLEASE FOLLOW THE STEPS BELOW:

  1. Close each application that is in use.
  2. Press the windows key.
  3. Click the power button.
  4. Choose between the following; Sleep, Restart & Shutdown.
  5. Click shutdown.
  6. Wait for the system to power itself down.

IF LAZY, HERE'S THE SHORTCUT:

  1. Click anywhere in the desktop.
  2. Press alt+F4 in the keyboard
  3. Use arrowkeys to choose between; Restart, Shutdown & Sleep.
  4. Press "Enter" key.
  5. Wait for the system to power itself down.
